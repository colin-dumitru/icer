Personas Section (minimum 4 personas recommended)

Type: Primary

Name: Daniel Ionescu

Background
  • Date of Birth: 12/05/1962
  • Gender: Male
  • Location: Iasi, Romania
  • Work place: Global Logistic, Engineer
  • School: "Alexandru Ioan Cuza" University
  • Technology Level: Intermediate
Main Points

Daniel Ionescu is a 51 year old engineer from Iasi, Romania, currently working at Global Logistics. He graduated from "Alexandru Ioan Cuza" University and he considers music a very important aspect of his life.

His musical preferences are well defined and has always required an organizational system for his playlists. In acquiring it, he uses his own computer and different websites created for this purpose. He has an average experience with computers and he can understand English at a basic level.

Goals
  • Practical Goals:
    • Avoid spending too much time in organizing his collection by playlists.
    • The ability to view his song history.
    • Discover similar artists/bands to the ones he enjoys listening to.
    Personal Goals:
    • Be prepared for every occasion with the appropriate playlist.
Frustrations and Pain Points

Some of the difficulties the user has with the product.

  • He has some difficulties in using features of the application.
  • He does not understand every English term used in the application, but manages to do what he needs with it.
  • Daniel finds it difficult to keep up with the increasing number of artists and genres.
  • Some of the suggested artists and songs are not to his taste.
Detailed Description
  • Daniel Ionescu is a 51 year old engineer who has been working at Global Logistics for 25 years. He became an important asset for his company soon after being employed, his hard work and dedication being recognized at a very early stage by his co-workers. Although his English level is not advanced, he is able to communicate when necessary, after taking some courses. He started using a computer later in life and still finds it difficult to use certain features of different websites.
  • During his student life, he was always passionate about music but he did not have the means to organize a collection it the way he wanted too, giving the lack of a computer and the difficulty to find the albums on the market.
  • His tastes in music were well defined since childhood and still remembers the first concert he attended with his father in Germany. He was very influenced by that experience which led him to develop an appreciation for rock music.
  • His favorite bands are Pink Floyd and Led Zeppelin and he is proud to say he has imprinted his musical taste on his two children, Alexandra and Marius.
  • Although his music preferences have not changed since college, he is open to discovering new artists, but does not always understand the new trends and genres that are constantly appearing on the market.
  • Daniel believes it is important to always have "smart" playlists at hand, suitable for every occasion and party. He likes to be in charge with the music at every gathering with friends and family.
  • Daniel is also interested in concerts in Romania, not being able to travel that often to watch his favorite bands play abroad. He considers the music industry to be very important and he always wants to be informed about the latest events. In order to do that, he checks different websites of the bands, but would like to be able to have a single source of information.
  • Besides being an engineer and a music passionate, he is also a husband to Ioana and has two children, who have always shared his interest in the 80's, early 90's songs, calling them "oldies but goldies".
References

Scenarios

Create multiple playlists suitable for different occasions

1. Daniel wants to be able to gather songs from his favorite artists in to one or more playlists.

2. He searches for a specific song.

3. He selects the "Add to Playlist" option.

4. If Daniel does not want to use any of his existent playlists, he has the possibility to create a new one.


View song history

1. Daniel wants to always be able to come back and view his song history.

2. He selects the "View History" option on the top page.

3. He is able to view his recent played songs by date or by artist

4. He has the possibility to view the history by either the most recent or by the first song played.


Discover similar artists/bands

1. Daniel wants to find similar artists to those he enjoys listening to.

2. When searching for a song, he can immediately view a list of more tracks from the artist/band.

3. He can also select the "Suggestions" options and he can view songs similar by genre.
